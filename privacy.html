Privacy Policy

Peak District Ethels App (by Hillbagging Apps)

Your data belongs to you

We built this app to help you explore the hills — not to gather data about you. Any information generated while using the app (such as the hills you've visited, the dates and times of your walks, your location, or your in-app preferences) is stored locally on your device for your own use. It is never transmitted to us or shared with any third party.

No personal data collected

We do not collect, process, or store any personal data as defined under the UK Data Protection Act 2018 or the UK GDPR. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no servers receiving information about you or your activity.

Location

The app uses your device's GPS to calculate your distance from hill summits. This happens entirely on your device — your location is never sent anywhere.

Contact

If you have any questions about how the app handles your data, please get in touch.
